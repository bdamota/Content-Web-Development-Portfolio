KickBack! is a web application that uses third-party APIs to generate randomized movie and recipe suggestions based on the user’s input of movie genre and cuisine type. The motivation for the project was to make it easier for people to stay in and spend quality time with their loved ones during the pandemic. I co-developed this concept with three other classmates in my UCLA Coding Bootcamp cohort.

Concept: Alex Chigas, Rochelle Ruiz
Team: Briana daMota, Alex Chigas, Rochelle Ruiz, Jackelin Salinas
My Roles: UX Design Lead, Writer, Front-end Developer
Time Period: 2 Weeks (July 2020)
Design Tools: Figma, Pen & Paper
Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, OMDB API, MovieDB API, Spoonacular API
