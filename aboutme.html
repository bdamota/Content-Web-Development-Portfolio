about me

I have a passion for people and telling diverse stories that reflect both our unique and shared experiences. I've spent the last ten years working with clients to create authentic and compelling content that resonates with general market and multicultural audiences. 

I'm currently enrolled in an intensive Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp at UCLA Extension and a UX Design program through Udacity, with the goal of gaining the skills for polished, fully responsive, interactive and accessible front end web design and development.

When I'm not working or trying to learn something new, I most recently can be found in my garden, cooking, or walking my dog around the neighborhood.
